A whole page for your eyes

Your Hazel Green Eyes

Your eyes are my favorite place to get lost. This page is just a tiny list of all the things I love about them – and about the beautiful soul behind them.

Their Color

They’re this perfect mix of hazel and green – sometimes soft and warm like afternoon light, sometimes deep and mysterious like a forest after rain. I could spend forever just memorizing every shade.

The Way They Glow

When you’re excited, your eyes actually sparkle. They light up a room more than any lamp or screen ever could, and I always end up smiling just because you’re happy.

How They Soften

When you’re tired, shy, or feeling a little down, your eyes turn gentle and soft. I see so much kindness and vulnerability in them, and it makes me want to protect you even more.

How They Listen

When I talk, you really listen. Your eyes stay on me, patient and focused, and in that moment I feel like I’m the only person in the world that matters to you.

How They Love

There is a special look you give me – that soft, quiet, “I love you” look without words. I replay that look in my head on the hardest days, and it always makes everything better.

How They See Me

You see the best in me, even when I don’t. Your hazel green eyes make me feel chosen, safe, and truly loved – and that is the greatest gift you could ever give me.
