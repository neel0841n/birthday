Little Love Letters

Here are a few small letters to you. You can change the text to make it even more personal, but I hope each one already feels like a hug in sentence form.

To the Birthday Girl

Today isn’t just another day on the calendar – it’s the day the world became softer, brighter, and a lot more beautiful because you were born. I’m so grateful that of all the people on this planet, I get to be the one standing beside you, celebrating you.

To Your Heart

Thank you for your patience, your warmth, your silliness, and your kindness. You always try your best, even when no one notices. I notice. I appreciate every small thing you do, even the ones you think don’t matter.

To Our Future

I don’t know exactly where life will take us, but I know this: I want your hand in mine for every chapter that’s coming. I want more birthdays, more late-night talks, more ordinary days that feel special just because you’re there.

To Your Hazel Green Eyes

When the world feels heavy, your eyes are my safe place. They remind me that I’m loved, seen, and chosen. I hope you always see yourself the way I see you: strong, gentle, and breathtaking in every possible way.
